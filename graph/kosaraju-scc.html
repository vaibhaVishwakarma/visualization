Complexity Analysis

Time Complexity: O(V + E)
The algorithm requires two full Depth-First Searches. Reversing the graph takes O(V + E). DFS processes every vertex and edge exactly once, yielding linear time complexity.

Space Complexity: O(V + E)
Space is used for the standard adjacency list adj, the transposed adjacency list adjT, the vis array, and the st Stack.
