Complexity Analysis

Time Complexity: O(V + E)
A single Depth-First Search traverses every vertex (V) and every edge (E) exactly once, finding all bridges in linear time.

Space Complexity: O(V + E)
Space is required to build the adjacency list `O(V + E)`, the call stack for DFS `O(V)`, and the tracking arrays `time[]`, `low[]`, and `visited[]` `O(V)`.
