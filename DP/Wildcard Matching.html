Reverse Mapping Formula

• If s[i] == p[j] || p[j] == '?':
    dp[i][j] = dp[i+1][j+1] (Look down-right)
• Else if p[j] == '*':
    dp[i][j] = dp[i+1][j] || dp[i][j+1] (Look down OR right)
