Interval Boundary Mechanics

• `a[i-1] * a[k] * a[j+1]`:
Because balloon `k` is chosen as the last balloon to pop inside range `[i, j]`, everything else within that scope has vanished. Its real-time neighbors are the outside boundary guards `i-1` and `j+1`.
